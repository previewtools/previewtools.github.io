What is Google Ads Preview?

Google Ads Preview is a vital tool for PPC (Pay-Per-Click) marketers and advertising professionals. It allows you to see exactly how your search ads will appear in Google search results on both desktop and mobile devices before you launch your campaigns.

Ad copy quality directly impacts your click-through rate (CTR) and campaign performance. By previewing your ads, you can test different headlines and descriptions to find the most compelling combination. You can also verify that your ad extensions (like site links, callouts, and structured snippets) display correctly.

This tool is essential for ensuring your ads are professional, clear, and optimized for both user experience and search engine requirements. Perfect for digital marketing agencies, in-house marketing teams, and individual consultants managing Google Ads campaigns.

How to Use Google Ads Preview

1

Enter Headlines

Enter up to 3 headlines for your search ad (max 30 characters each). Google rotates these headlines in search results.

2

Add Descriptions

Write up to 2 description lines (max 90 characters each). These describe your offer and call-to-action.

3

Set Display URL

Enter your display URL as it should appear in the search result (max 35 characters). This is the visible URL, different from the actual landing page.

4

Preview on Both Devices

See how your ad appears on Google desktop and mobile search results. Note how headlines and descriptions truncate on mobile.

5

Add Extensions (Optional)

Preview ad extensions like site links, callout extensions, and structured snippets. See how they enhance your ad.
