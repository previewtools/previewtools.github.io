What is OpenGraph Preview?

OpenGraph Preview is a crucial tool for anyone sharing content on social media. It allows you to see exactly how your links will appear when shared on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and other social platforms that use Open Graph Protocol.

When you share a URL on social media, platforms use OpenGraph (OG) meta tags to determine what content to display - the image, title, description, and more. If these tags aren't properly configured, your shared content may look unprofessional or fail to capture the attention of potential visitors. This tool helps you optimize these tags for maximum social impact.

Whether you're sharing blog posts, product pages, or any web content, the OpenGraph Preview ensures your social shares look professional and drive clicks. Perfect for content creators, marketers, web developers, and anyone serious about their social media presence.

How to Use OpenGraph Preview

1

Enter Your URL

Input the URL you want to preview. The tool fetches existing OpenGraph tags or lets you enter custom values for testing.

2

Customize OG Tags

Edit the title (35-65 characters), description (80-110 characters), and image URL. See real-time validation for each field.

3

Preview on All Platforms

View how your link appears on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and other social networks. Each platform has different display requirements.

4

Optimize Images

Check image dimensions, file size, and format. Ensure images are optimized for each social platform's requirements.

5

Get Recommendations

Receive actionable suggestions for improving your OG tags and metadata for better social sharing performance.
