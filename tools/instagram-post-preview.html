What is Instagram Post Preview?

Instagram Post Preview is a powerful tool that allows you to visualize your content exactly as it will appear to your followers on Instagram. Whether you're posting a regular feed photo, crafting a story, or uploading a reel, our tool shows you precisely how it will look on both mobile and desktop views.

This free tool helps you make informed decisions about your content before posting. You can check image quality, text visibility, caption length, and overall visual appeal. With Instagram's algorithm favoring high-quality, engaging content, previewing your posts can significantly improve your social media performance.

Perfect for content creators, social media managers, businesses, and influencers who want to maintain a consistent and professional appearance on their Instagram profiles.

How to Use Instagram Post Preview

1

Upload or Paste Content

Upload your image, text, or reel URL to the preview tool. You can paste content directly or upload files from your device. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, GIF, and video files.

2

Choose Preview Format

Select whether you want to preview a feed post, story, or reel. Each format has different dimensions and display requirements that our tool will simulate accurately.

3

Add Caption and Tags

Enter your caption, hashtags, and mentions. The tool shows how many characters you've used and provides recommendations for optimal engagement and reach.

4

Review on Mobile & Desktop

Switch between mobile and desktop views to see how your post appears on different devices. This ensures your content looks perfect for all your followers.

5

Get Suggestions

Receive AI-powered suggestions for improving engagement, including optimal posting times, hashtag recommendations, and caption length suggestions.
